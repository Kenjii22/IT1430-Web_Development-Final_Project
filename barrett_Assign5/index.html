About the City

Jubilife City is the first large city that a new trainer will run into when they start their journey. Being known as "The City of Joy," multiple businesses such as the Jubilife TV station, the Trainer School, the Global Trade Center, and the headquarters of the Pokétch Company.

There are 4 points of entry for the city in each of the cardinal directions. The south entrance connects to Route 202 followed by Sandgem Town. The east entrance connects to Route 203 and leads to Oreburgh City. The north entrance leads to Route 204 and Floaroma Town. The west entrance leads to Route 218 and Canalave City.

Why Jubilife?

I first started playing Pokémon when I was really young and have been following the franchise ever since. I thought that using one of the bigger cities from the first game I played of the series would be a good idea.
